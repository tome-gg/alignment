Refund Policy

Simple.

We keep our refund policy simple by distinguishing it into two: general refund policies and specific refund policies.

As a service business, our goal is to deliver the best value to our beloved customers, and to keep you focused on your training and personal growth while we empower you behind the scenes.

Specific Refund Policies: At the start of any service or program that you register to, we create a Apprenticeship Contract that details the specific terms of your refund policy tailored for the program that you are in. These are the primary terms of the refund policy. Anything not specified here relies on the general terms of the refund policy.

General Refund Policies: If you are unsatisfied with the service and are unsatisfied with the quality of mentorship and work that we do, inform your mentor first and provide feedback. If this does not work, inform our team immediately or email darren.sapalo@gmail.com. This initiates the refund process which takes usually 3-5 business days.

Acceptable Refund Cases: Consistent under-performance by your mentor (as described in your contract) are grounds for you to claim a refund. With valid grounds for requesting a refund, you may claim a full refund if you've only completed 30% of your program duration. Any progress after that, you can avail of a partial refund (graduated decrease as it nears to the end of the program).
