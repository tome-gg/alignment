What is mentorship?

At its core, mentorship is a relationship. Mentorship is about people.

At its highest form, mentorship is an intimate relationship. It is about connecting with people and uplifting them, taking part in their journey to a safe, sustainable, fully-lived, and meaningful life.

How do I make my relationships thrive?

All relationships are founded on a mutual agreement of trust and respect.

Just like in any kind of relationship, mentorship has its ups and downs, it experiences miscommunications, it evolves, it changes, it bears fruit, it dies, and a new life is born from the next seeds it sows. Just as a relationship requires hard work on both parties sharing in the responsibilities to make it thrive, relationships subsist on good communication and affording each other space to make mistakes.

We have a distinct method, style, and philosophy of mentorship as described in our Tome.gg Protocols. Feel free to check it out at your leisure.

Mentorship can be a scary thing to explore, especially if you have unanswered questions or fears and uncertainties that are overwhelming. Feel free to reach out to us and express your concerns or ask questions. Use a pseudonym and throwaway mailinator email if you prefer.
