Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2024

1. Introduction

Tome.gg ("we," "us," "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our products and services.

2. Information We Collect

A. Feedback Forms

When users provide feedback on coaching and mentoring sessions, we collect:

  • Timestamp of the session
  • Email address
  • Satisfaction rating
  • Likelihood of recommending the session
  • Valuable aspects of the session
  • Suggestions for improvement
  • Coach's understanding of user needs
  • Likelihood of participating in another session
  • Additional comments or feedback

B. Growth Journal

For apprentices registering for the Tome.gg program, we collect:

  • Journal entries (timestamped)
  • Objectives (short-term and long-term goals)
  • Observations (behavioral patterns, problems, feelings, challenges, desires)
  • Action items (start doing, stop doing, continue doing)

Apprentices own their growth journals but grant Tome.gg and designated Tome.gg mentors access to read and write on these documents. Sensitive information may be included but should remain confidential and related to personal and professional growth.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect personal information through:

  • Cookies
  • Third-party services
  • Forms on the Tome.gg website
  • Logging in on the website
  • Collaboration on Google Docs and GitHub repositories

4. Use of Personal Information

We use personal information to:

  • Collect feedback on our services
  • To personalize the coaching and mentorship and accountability programs
  • Improve overall user and customer experience
  • Help apprentices gain clarity in their growth journey
  • Help apprentices define clear, measurable SMART goals for their mentorship program
  • Tailor recommendations and suggestions based on behavior and learning stages
  • Conduct marketing with user consent (e.g., sharing anonymized case studies)

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Mentors: Personal information from growth journals is shared with designated mentors on the Tome.gg platform with the apprentice's explicit consent.
  • Third Parties: We do not sell personal information to third parties.
  • Internal Use: Aggregated, anonymized data is analyzed to improve user experience.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Locations: Personal information is stored on cloud services such as Google Cloud and AWS.
  • Security Measures: We use HTTPS, SSL, and best practices for password strength and access controls. Regular security audits are conducted biannually.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Modify and correct inaccurate information
  • Delete their data
  • Restrict access to their data
  • Export their data

Users can exercise these rights through:

  • Google Docs sharing settings
  • GitHub repository access management
  • Contacting our support team to request feedback data to be removed

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze user behavior and engagement

Users can manage their cookie preferences through their browser settings.

9. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing personal information includes:

  • User consent
  • Performance of a contract (mentorship agreement between apprentice, mentor, and Tome.gg)
  • Legitimate interests (improving services)

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law, typically up to two years.

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We will notify users of changes to this Privacy Policy through email notifications and updates on our website.
