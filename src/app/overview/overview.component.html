How do I negotiate mentorship terms?

It can be difficult and overwhelming to figure out what your options are when negotiating different terms with your mentor.

Tome.gg built negotiation protocols using Solana smart contracts, to enable you and your mentor to quickly iterate your negotiations, explore what can be a mutually beneficial arrangement, or help you both discern whether to disengage.

What does transfer learning mean?

In Tome.gg, we leverage transfer learning to teach our students rapidly. It leverages a shared schema, model, or understanding to communicate quickly and efficiently. Simply put, it is what we do when we make analogies.
