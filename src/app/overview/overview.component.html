Mentorship is a very engaged and personal process, and it takes time to discover what mentorship parameters work for you. Tome.gg has variety of mentors in its mentor pool.

How do I negotiate mentorship terms?

It can be difficult and overwhelming to figure out what your options are when negotiating different terms with your mentor.

Tome.gg built negotiation protocols using Solana smart contracts, to enable you and your mentor to quickly iterate your negotiations, explore what can be a mutually beneficial arrangement, or help you both discern whether to disengage.

What does a Tome.gg Growth Journal look like?

Your growth journal content and metacontent is stored in YAML format in a public GitHub repository owned by you, where you have full authority to grant and revoke read/write access to past, present, and future mentors that you will engage with. This growth journal is what the smart contract interacts with automatically to evaluate your performance.

Check out our growth journal's generated public template to see how Tome.gg mentors are in involved in guiding you in your direction setting and growth- personalized specifically for you.

What does transfer learning mean?

In Tome.gg, we leverage transfer learning to teach our students rapidly. It leverages a shared schema, model, or understanding to communicate quickly and efficiently. Simply put, it is what we do when we make analogies.
