Rapid coaching

Short. More signal. Less noise.

Who benefits the most from rapid coaching?

  • People who have a clear idea of what they know and don't know.
  • People who feel confident in their ability to communicate their specific needs, ask precise questions, and provide a sufficiently rich but concise contextual summary of their problem.
  • People with strong communication skills, and confidence in their ability to explain.
  • People who journal their investigation/analysis but have gotten stuck and not sure where they should go or what they should do next.
  • People who don't need to be hand-held in solving a problem- they just need to be pointed in the right direction.

What kinds of results does the rapid coaching achieve?

  • Quick consultations- immediate feedback.
  • More economical option if pre-work and post-work is performed effectively and efficiently; but requires greater communication skill.
  • Concise.
