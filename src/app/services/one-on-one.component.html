Personalized. To your taste. More you.

Who benefits the most from 1-on-1 coaching?

  • People who don't know what their professional options are and need time and details in exploring career options and focused support in evaluating different options.
  • People who feel like they struggle to learn on their own and unsure about their feedback mechanisms
  • People who have low levels of confidence, wondering 'what am I doing' and 'am I doing this right'?
  • People who want to achieve clarity in directions in their career and professional life, and determining the work-life integration that suits them best.
  • People who have felt a lack of support from team leads or in-company mentors- or have felt that these authority figures lack the humility to acknowledge their shortcomings.
  • People who feel challenged or disappointed by the level of specificity, detail, and rigor from their existing mentors; feeling the need to seek access to greater mentors.

What kinds of results does the 1-on-1 coaching achieve?

  • Gaining clarity on one's values and important goals- enabling greater strategy and optimization in learning.
  • Gaining a broader picture in the career navigation in the tech space.
  • Acquiring the necessary technological and professional literacy to recognize one's own mistakes and develop independent learning habits and skills.
  • Being clear about the work-life balance that suits them.
  • Learning to develop precise communication skills, in working with their mentors, team-leads, CEOs, CTOs, peers, and juniors.
  • A more peaceful life that isn't plagued by impostor syndrome and dreading making mistakes.
