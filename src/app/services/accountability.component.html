Accountability Program

More action. Less thinking.

Who benefits the most from accountability programs?

  • People who have already have a clear understanding of their problem and solutions. They struggle with the 'doing' part.
  • People who think "I know what the problem is and I know what I'm supposed to do. I just don't do it."
  • People who are clear with their motivations for developing these software engineering skills, but struggle to get started with or completing work.
  • People who struggle with the skill of estimation and practicing confidence levels.

What kinds of results does the accountability programs achieve?

  • You learn from your mentors how to plan and set goals better.
  • You achieve the manageable, realistic goals that you set.
  • Begin with baby steps.
