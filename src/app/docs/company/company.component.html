Vision

Developing high-quality mentors ensures the transfer of practical knowledge and real-world experience to the next generation of tech professionals. Nuturing a culture of continuous learning is important, and it is a noble undertaking to live out our principles as role models, advocates, sponsors, coaches, and mentors. This builds resilient, skilled individuals capable of adapting to evolving technological landscapes. The ultimate end goal is promoting long-term sustainability in tech and startups by nurturing talent and reducing skill gaps.

If you are interested in tools that enable scalable mentorship, please reach out and join our journey with Tome.gg in achieving highly-personalized mentorship at scale.

The Team

Tome.gg is composed of a lean team of 5 Filipinos, with big dreams of enabling scalable, hyper-personalized mentorship.

Altogether, we have the expertise of digital marketing, brand strategy, operations management and business development, instructional materials design, technology, coaching and mentorship.
