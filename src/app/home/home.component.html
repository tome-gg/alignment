Our Coaching Process

Discovery & Directions Deep Dive (D4)

The first step in our program is a discovery process. Here, our primary objective is to enable you to gain clarity.

We do this through a structure process of exploration, while your coach/mentor journals the information that you are sharing. This habit of precisely writing your thoughts down is demonstrated for you, to help you develop the necessary habit of writing to collect your thoughts. A one-page executive summary is prepared for you and sent afterwards.

  • Fill up registration form
  • Book a coaching session schedule
  • Define the scope of coaching during the session

After the session

After the coaching session, we send over the following:

  1. A one-page detailed report of the discussion. This also acts as the first page of your growth journal.
  2. A link to the 45-60 minute recorded coaching session, for your later review and study.
  3. An email that recaps an executive summary of our discussions along with your action items.
