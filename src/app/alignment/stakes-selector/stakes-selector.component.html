Stake

Now that you've specified your Session Frequency preference, let's propose some stake.

A "stake" is what you offer as a guarantee that you will do your share of the responsibilities. If you fail to fulfill your end of the bargain, then you lose your stake. The same goes for your mentor, should they fail to regularly attend your meetings.
