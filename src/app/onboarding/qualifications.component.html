What are the characteristics of a good apprentice?

We talk about this topic in length in various areas. Temporarily, we shall use the following description as a summary of a good apprentice.

Ultimately, a good apprentice is a curious practitioner of intellectual humility, is committed to their growth and direction, and is generous with their learnings.

Characteristics

Other people talk about what it means to be a good apprentice too. I'll place resource materials of accounts of good apprenticeships here.

  • Their curiosity drives them to read and ask questions a lot
  • Their intellectual humility enables them to ask for feedback and opens them to better self-awareness
  • Their generosity in sharing or teaching what they've learned demands that their communication reach high standards

You may not have all of these characteristics as of the moment, but that is fine. What's important is you accept where you are currently in your progress and that you make a commitment to yourself for your growth. Choose a path, and take your steps forward.
