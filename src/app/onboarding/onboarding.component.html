Growth Inventory: Explainer

The following section is a free assessment called a Growth Inventory, which reveals to you your primary areas of opportunity for growth as a mid- to senior-level software engineer.

It is a 5-minute screening assessment that is composed of 25 multiple-choice questions that focus solely on your perspective about your work as a software engineer, your perspectives on your growth and communications, and your assessment of your confidence and competence level.

Once completed, you will receive a one-page summary of results that highlights your areas of growth in three areas: (1) communications, (2) best practices in growth, and (3) navigating complex and challenging scenarios.

To receive a deeper explanation about each aspect of your areas of opportunity for growth, you may provide your email and we can send you our five-page explainer tailored to your results.

This data is collected for the primary purpose of academic and market research in understanding the challenges in software engineering apprenticeship and mentorship. Read about our data privacy policy.
