Hi there! I'm Darren, the Founder of Tome.gg. Welcome to our coaching and mentoring platform for software engineers!

This guide explains more about what results and outcomes you'll get by going through the Tome.gg program.

What Tome.gg does for you

Our goal in Tome.gg is to help you optimize, personalize, and accelerate your learning journey.

We do this in a variety of ways:

  1. low-touch experiences (i.e. not so personalized);
  2. high-touch experiences

Your responsibility free

At the lowest levels of personalization, Tome.gg asserts that you, as the apprentice, must begin with accepting this hard truth: that all external help (friends, family, colleagues, bosses, mentors) will only be effective on a strong foundation of your responsibility, accountability, and commitment to your personal growth. This means that crafting your legendary tome of knowledge that is attuned to you begins with your ownership of this responsibility.

You will do most of the legwork in reading a lot, writing and rewriting, and practicing your skills and communications with others.

Your resources free

Tome.gg provides information and guidance.

Information - Tome.gg provides low-touch, personalized education by making highly relevant stories in tech and startups within reach through the Tome.gg Library.

Guidance - Tome.gg supports this high-accountability, high-ownership mindset by providing guidance on best ways of learning through the Tome.gg Protocols. You can read these instructions and practice it on your own or with a couple of friends.

Your community alpha

The next level of personalization comes with the Discord community: The Gaming Classroom.

As a community we gather together like-minded folks to talk about technology, education and learning, and casual and competitive gaming.

As a community, we learn and grow together, hold each other accountable, and push each other to our best capabilities. We do this every step of the way with trust and respect as the foundations of our relationships.

The community of Tome.gg is always free to join. However, a subset of the Tome.gg community will have private access to services, tools, and learning resources. These resources will be accessible temporarily for free while we are currently in the alpha stage, but will later be made accessible only for Patrons: community members that are contributing to Tome.gg's platform and ecosystem through a monthly premium subscription.

Your mentors

At the highest level of personalization, you get your own personal mentor that provides either 1-on-1 coaching, or group coaching (3 apprentices at a time).

Coaching and mentoring is available as a paid service, and is sometimes made available through sponsorships and community events. Keep in touch in Discord to receive notifications on community events!
